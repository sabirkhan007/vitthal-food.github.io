About Us

VITTHAL FOODS AND BEVERAGES (Brand – RadhaShree Namkeen), based in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand is a renowned name in the field of Namkeen & Food Items. With exquisite taste, impeccable quality, and competitive prices, we have entrenched ourselves as one of the most preferred manufacturers of Indian Traditional Namkeen and Food Items such as Fryums, Rings, Namkeen, etc. with the combination of fair business principles, efficient service, and solid products, we expect a successful and growth-oriented future. Due to the manufacturing of excellent quality products, we have established a strong reputation in the market. All our food products are made of quality raw materials. We maintain the highest food quality, safety, and purity standards.

We have been issued FSSAI License, ISO 9001:2015 (QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM), and ISO 22000:2018 (FOOD SAFETY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM)

We VITTHAL FOODS AND BEVERAGES launched in 2021. We are a young entrant in the field of Beverages, Snacks & Namkeen. But we have a strong, dynamic, and experienced team of human resources who work hard with full dedication. Our team consists of Food technologists, Operation and Quality experts along with a hardworking sales team who ensure proper positioning and distribution of our quality products to every corner of the state. It’s a fun-filled and tasty journey of our founders, blending tastes and traditions and our founders have been into other food products manufacturing for the last 30 years. We believe in bringing you the best and something new and a unique blend of products always and hence we always keep our research and new product development in the ON mode.

Unlike others, we don’t claim to have an ultramodern manufacturing facility. We are equipped with semi-modern and automatic manufacturing facilities. We believe in going Back to Basics and restarting the traditional taste of namkeen using the traditional methods of preparation. We don’t compromise on quality. We serve you a mouth-watering, crispy, and fresh range of hygienically packed assortments.

Being a team of foodies ourselves, we care for your health as well. This is why making less oily yet exceptionally tasty products is our prime cause. Our Corn Items are BAKED and NOT FRIED as this range is majorly liked and consumed by kids

Mission & Vision

We aim to spread RadhaShree Namkeen to the mass and class of Indian society. We would be happy to see every single person eating and enjoying RadhaShree brand food products and to become the market leader in the food business by providing quality, healthy, and tasty packaged food to our customers at a reasonable price

We wish to win space in the mouth and hearts of the customers
